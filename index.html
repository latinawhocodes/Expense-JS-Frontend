Flatiron Expensify

Do you keep asking yourself where all your money is going? Tired of wondering and keeping hundreds of old receipts? Welcome to Flatiron Expensify! The perfect app to help you keep track of your spending and get you back on track to saving up for your dream vacation!

In the form below, select from the available categories and enter the amount of the transaction. You can select a date from the dropdown menu and enter a short optional description.
